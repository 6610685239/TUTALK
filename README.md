## Key Features

### TU Talk — Campus Social Feed
A community space where TU students can post, share opinions, and spark conversations across university topics.

- **Post & Share:** Publish text or image posts for the campus community
- **React & Engage:** Like, Repost, and Comment on others' content
- **Stay Connected:** Follow threads and join ongoing discussions

---

### TU Alert — Campus Safety Notifications
A real-time alert system that keeps students informed about important events and emergencies on campus.

- **Instant Alerts:** Get notified about accidents, emergencies, and critical campus events
- **Zone-Based Grouping:** Alerts are categorized by specific areas within the university

---

### TU Review — Rate Everything on Campus
A review platform letting students share honest opinions about places and experiences at TU.

- **Food Reviews:** Rate and review restaurants and canteens across campus
- **Dormitory Reviews:** Share your experience living in TU dorms
- **Teaching Reviews:** Rate courses and instructors to help future students
- **Community Ratings:** See aggregated scores and opinions from fellow students

---

### TU Party — Find Your Crew
A social matchmaking feature to find people for activities, hangouts, and events.

- **Create Events:** Post upcoming activities and invite others to join
- **Find Participants:** Discover people looking for the same activities
- **Activity Details:** Specify venue, time, and requirements for each event
- **Join & Go:** Browse open activities and request to join in one tap
